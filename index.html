What we do?

Blood Case Management

Blood for Nepal team is actively managing blood cases. Many patient parties face difficulties in finding donors in time.

Awareness for Pregnant Women

Educating on managing blood supply before childbirth and advising the arrangement of at least four donors in advance.

Blood Donation Program

To ensure blood availability, we organize donation programs across the country. This addresses situations where blood banks or donors are unavailable in time.

Blood Donation Awareness

We promote blood donation and educate people on its benefits. We also provide strategies for managing blood needs, especially for operations and deliveries.

Ghardailo Blood Donation and Awareness Program

We started conducting ghardailo donation program since COVID-19 has became pandemic. We have seen the people wants to donate but they are unable to reach in blood bank. In that case we conduct program in their own area to facilitate the donors and collect the blood. Also, we will continue doing this program further too.

Message From Blood Donor

You don’t need to be a doctor to save life, you can save life by donatingblood too.” I love to serve the people in need. The best way of the service is to save life of the people by donating blood. In every three months, I donate blood to fulfil my social responsibility and passion. My blood group is “A Negative” which is rare in Nepal. When I get connected with Blood For Nepal (BFN) via their online form, they start to call me to donate fresh blood for the patients in every three months. It makes me more responsible. BFN is connecting link between needy and blood donors. It is doing incredible job for the mankind.
